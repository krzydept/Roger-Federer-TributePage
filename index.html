Main facts about Roger - the Swiss Maestro / Fedex

Roger Federer born in August 8 1981 is a Swiss proffessional tennis player. He is currently (march 2018) ranked world No. 1 in men's singles tennis by ATP while re-entering the top after his ten victory at the 2017 Australian Open.

Federer has won 20 Grand Slam singles titles - the most in history for a male player and has held the world No.1 spot in ATP rankings for a record total of 306 weeks, including 237 consecutive weeks.
